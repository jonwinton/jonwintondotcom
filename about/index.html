About Me

I'm Jon Winton, a front-end developer in Brooklyn, New York. When I'm not making stuff for the Internet, I spend my time rock climbing and traveling and eating.

When it comes to web development I'm most passionate about accessibility/semantic markup, JavaScript (Node & Browser), and data visualization. In my free time I also teach high schoolers the basics of front-end developement with ScriptEd.

If you want to talk shop or know where to find a breakfast taco in NYC hit me up on Twitter.
